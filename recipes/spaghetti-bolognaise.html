Spaghetti Bologniase Recipe

Ingredients

  • 350g San Remo spaghetti
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped
  • 4 slices prosciutto, chopped
  • 1 tsp Masterfoods garlic
  • 500g Coles Australian 4 Star beef mince
  • 500g btl Dolmio Extra Bolognese pasta sauc
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 2 tbsp basil, finely chopped
  • Basil leaves, to serve
  • Parmesan, finely grated to serve

Steps

  1. Cook San Remo Spaghetti in a large saucepan of boiling water following packet directions or until al dente. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the onion, carrot, celery, prosciutto and garlic. Cook, stirring, for 5 mins or until the onion softens. Add the mince and cook, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up any lumps, for 5 mins or until the mince changes colour and is cooked through.
  3. Stir in the pasta sauce. Add the rosemary sprig. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook, covered, for 5 mins.
  4. Remove rosemary sprig. Stir in chopped basil. Divide spaghetti among serving bowls. Top with sauce and basil leaves. Serve with parmesan.
